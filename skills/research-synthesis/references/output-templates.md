## MECE Guidance

Aim for a structure that is as close to MECE as the topic allows:

- separate dimensions that answer different questions
- avoid restating the same conclusion in multiple sections
- acknowledge overlap when the domain does not permit a perfectly clean split

Do not force a rigid taxonomy if the reader would understand the topic better through a more pragmatic grouping.
