# Output Checklist

Approve a slide only after all checks below pass:

- the slide type matches the content
- title and body have clear hierarchy
- content density looks balanced rather than sparse or crowded
- all important elements remain inside the safety margin
- no text overlaps another text-bearing element
- no object collisions make the slide feel cramped
- repeated objects share clean alignment lines
- card, column, or table spacing is consistent
- body text remains readable
- fonts follow the approved fallback strategy
- no garbled characters, unsupported symbols, or broken mixed-language rendering appear
- the slide can be scanned quickly without confusion
