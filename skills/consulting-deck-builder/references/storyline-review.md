# Storyline Review

Use this reference to review horizontal logic.
Read only the action titles in sequence.

## What the Titles Should Convey

A reader who sees only the titles should understand:
- current situation
- core problem or opportunity
- supporting evidence path
- proposed resolution
- decision or call to action

## Review Dimensions

### Narrative Flow

Check:
- the opening slides establish context cleanly
- each title follows naturally from the previous one
- no key logic step is missing
- no title is redundant
- the flow builds toward a decision

### SCR Alignment

Map the deck to:
- situation
- complication
- resolution
- decision or CTA

If any bucket is missing, identify the gap.

### Audience Fit

Check:
- expected objections are answered somewhere in the flow
- the depth matches the audience
- the audience can decide by the second-to-last slide
