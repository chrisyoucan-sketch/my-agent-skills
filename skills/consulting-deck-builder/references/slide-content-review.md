# Slide Content Review

Use this reference to review vertical logic and factual quality after slide bodies are drafted.

## Review Dimensions

### Vertical Logic

Check:
- every bullet proves the title
- nothing belongs on a different slide
- strongest evidence appears first
- the body makes the title feel inevitable

### Data Accuracy

Check:
- numbers match the provided source material
- calculations are plausible and consistent
- no fabricated data appears
- the same metric uses the same unit throughout

### Completeness

Check:
- all slides are present
- all slides have speaker notes
- factual claims include source tags
- callouts add insight, not decoration

### Language Quality

Check:
- no banned buzzwords
- no em dashes
- one language register throughout
- detail level fits a presentation, not a report

## Score Bands

- 95-100: presentation-ready
- 90-94: minor adjustments
- 80-89: logic or evidence gaps
- below 80: major rewrite needed
