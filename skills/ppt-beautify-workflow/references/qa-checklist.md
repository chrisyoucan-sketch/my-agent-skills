# QA Checklist

Approve the deck only after these checks pass:

- the visual system is consistent across cover, section, content, and data pages
- every slide has one clear primary layout type
- important content stays inside the outer safety margin
- title and body hierarchy is visually obvious
- body text is readable without strain
- no text overlaps another text-bearing element
- no shape or image crowds adjacent text
- repeated cards, columns, or labels align to shared edges or baselines
- tables and charts have enough breathing room
- the deck does not alternate randomly between unrelated page styles
- no slide feels obviously underfilled unless it is a cover or section divider
- no slide feels compressed edge-to-edge to avoid splitting content
- fonts are stable and unlikely to fall back poorly on Windows
- any auto-fix changes were visually rechecked afterward
